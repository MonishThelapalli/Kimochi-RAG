Title: Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave Entitlement
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of Earned Leave (EL) and 12 days of Casual/Sick Leave (CSL) per calendar year. Leave accrues monthly. Probationary employees (first 3 months) can only avail CSL.

2. Leave Application Process
Leaves must be applied for via the 'Kimochi-HR' portal at least 3 days in advance for EL. CSL can be applied for on the same day in case of emergencies, but manager notification via Slack is mandatory by 9:30 AM.

3. Carry Forward Policy
Up to 10 days of EL can be carried forward to the next year. CSL does not carry forward and lapses on December 31st. Unused EL above 10 days can be encashed during the March payroll cycle.

4. Maternity and Paternity Leave
Maternity leave is 26 weeks as per statutory norms. Paternity leave is 2 weeks, to be availed within 2 months of the child's birth.
