Title: Holiday Calendar 2025

1. Mandatory Holidays (Fixed)
- New Year's Day: January 1
- Republic Day: January 26
- Independence Day: August 15
- Gandhi Jayanti: October 2
- Labor Day: May 1
- Christmas: December 25

2. Floating Holidays
Employees may choose any 4 optional holidays from the regional list (e.g., Diwali, Eid, Thanksgiving, Holi). These must be declared in the HR portal by January 31st of the year.

3. Working Weekends
We follow a 5-day work week (Monday to Friday). In exceptional cases involving critical deployments, weekend work may be required, for which compensatory off (Comp-Off) will be credited.
