Title: Expense Reimbursement Policy

1. Reimbursable Expenses
Employees can claim reimbursement for business-related expenses such as client machinery, software subscriptions (approved by IT), and team lunches (monthly cap).

2. Limits
- Team Lunch: $30 per person.
- Internet Bill (for non-WFH allowance claims): Up to $50/month.
- Learning & Development: $1000/year for courses and books.

3. Filing Process
All expenses must be filed on Expensify within 30 days of the transaction. Receipts are mandatory for any claim above $10. Approvals are routed to the direct manager and then Finance.

4. Payout Cycle
Approved claims are processed on the 15th and 30th of every month.
