Title: New Employee Onboarding Checklist

1. Day 1
- Collect laptop and access badge from IT.
- Attend HR orientation session (10:00 AM).
- Set up email signature and Slack profile.

2. Week 1
- Complete mandatory compliance training (Data Privacy, POSH).
- Schedule 1:1 meetings with immediate team members.
- Gain access to necessary GitHub repos and Jira boards.

3. Month 1
- Define OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) with the manager.
- Complete the "Kimochi Way" culture workshop.
