Title: Employee Benefits Overview

1. Health Insurance
Comprehensive medical insurance cover of $500,000 for the employee, spouse, and two children. Parents can be added with a co-pay.

2. Wellness
- Gym Reimbursement: 50% off monthly membership fees (up to $50).
- Mental Health: Free access to Headspace and 5 free counselling sessions per year via our EAP partner.

3. Stock Options
All full-time employees are granted ESOPs (Employee Stock Option Plan) vested over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.
