Title: Code of Conduct

1. Professionalism
Employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind (verbal, physical, or visual) has a zero-tolerance policy.

2. Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose any external business interests or moonlighting activities to HR. Running a competing business is strictly prohibited.

3. Social Media
Employees should not post confidential company information on social media. When expressing personal opinions, clearly state that they do not represent the company.
