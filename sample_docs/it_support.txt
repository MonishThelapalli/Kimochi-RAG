Title: IT Support Escalation Matrix

1. Standard Support
For standard issues (password reset, software install), file a ticket on Jira Service Desk.
SLA: 24 hours.

2. Urgent Support
For hardware failure or access blockage preventing work, call the IT Helpdesk Hotline (+1-800-KIMOCHI) or visit the IT Desk on floor 3.
SLA: 2 hours.

3. Escalation Matrix
- Level 1: IT Support Analyst
- Level 2: IT Operations Manager (escalate if SLA breached by 50%)
- Level 3: CTO (only for system-wide outages)
