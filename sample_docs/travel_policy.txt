Title: Corporate Travel Policy

1. Booking Classes
- Flights: Economy class for domestic and international flights under 6 hours. Premium Economy for flights over 6 hours. Business class requires VP approval.
- Hotels: Up to $150/night for domestic, $250/night for international.

2. Daily Allowance (Per Diem)
Employees traveling for work are entitled to a per diem of $75 (domestic) or $100 (international) to cover meals and incidentals. This is a flat rate and does not require receipts.

3. Booking Platform
All travel must be booked via the 'Kimochi-Travel' portal (powered by Navan) to ensure corporate rates and insurance coverage.
