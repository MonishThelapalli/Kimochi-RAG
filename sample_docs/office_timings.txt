Title: Office Timings

1. Standard Timing
Our office is open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Monday through Friday. Standard working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM with a one-hour lunch break.

2. Access Badge
Employees must wear their access badge at all times. Visitors must sign into the guest register at reception.

3. Late Stay
Employees working past 9:00 PM are eligible for a reimbursed Uber/Lyft ride home and a dinner allowance of $20.
